Gravity

This is a two-player, deep-space shooting game.

Each player controls a spaceship. They take it in turns shooting at their opponent. When it is your turn, your ship will be circled in green. To fire, hold down the mouse button. The power will start building. When it reaches the amount you want, release the mouse button. A missile will be launched towards your mouse cursor. It will bend around the nearby planets’ gravitational fields, so hitting your opponent will be tricky.

If you can’t find a path to your opponent’s ship, you might need to blast your way through a planet or two. Don’t worry, nobody much lives there.

Click anywhere to start.
